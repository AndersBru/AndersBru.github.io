About

Anders is a composer, specializing in creating cinematic and epic music for your trailer. With a long and diverse background, he is ready for any task. From dark, moody and eerie soundscapes, to heroic and uplifting orchestral music, Anders has can craft the perfect score for your next blockbuster trailer. He has a long list of AAA-trailers under his belt and an impressive list of collaborators, and does not limit his music to just the trailer industry. The world's leading EDM-producers and pop-acts all flock to Anders' music for use in their liveshows or custom promotional material. Anders is open for any request, so don't hesitate with contacting him!
